IATSAT INDIAN MISSION 2035

MISSION 2035

We IATSAT has come up with the holistic Mission of IATSAT INDIA 2035.With this mission of Empowering India with exponential growth of resource based learning, based on a sense of developing Economic independency in education.

How our mission will work with schools?

IATSAT is developing fully automated online teaching platform. It will help school to have best teachers training & resources available all time. Our mission is to connect school through satellite so that students can attend the classes even if they don’t have full faculties. There will be dedicated subject matter experts team who will help schools to bring innovation in study. Behind our success IATSAT is having 500 employees for Research & Development who will be directly connected to the schools. We have vision to create best learning experience for our Government school through parallel education system which will help student in near future to achieve their dreams. We are also creating free online Resources platform from students can access free quality courses material.
