IATSAT Global

Major Problems

Test Format

There will be six sections in this Test:

  1. Listening
  2. Numeric
  3. Reasoning
  4. Verbal Ability
  5. General Knowledge
  6. Life Skill

Duration of the test:

There won't be any negative marking however test must be completed in 2.30 hours and each section must be completed within time frame. It will be fully recorded

Global Assessment

The Higher education system across the globe isn’t at right pace not right place. IATSAT global is developed through years of research & analysis of industry professional specially catering to the students looking for an education outside their home country. Since IATSAT is fully recorded and same recording can be provided to the universities or colleges to see that how candidates have done the test so they might be able to select their best candidates through this process plus personal interview. About Exam: To ensure that ISSAT global becomes the standard across the international lines, we have added several advantages for students appearing in the ISSAT global Exam. End to end Process Pre Mentorship – IATSAT global Exam – IATSAT global Exam Result – PI Round conducted by university – confirmation letter from university for Admission – Visa (self )- Up to 50% Scholarship confirmed by IATSAT global – Arrival in the university –Post Mentorship

The IATSAT Process

How to Apply?

To apply, you can register on our IATSAT website. Once the registration process is completed you will be automatically directed to our test Partner. Once the authentication is done, you will receive online an admit card where the exam center and date of examination will be mentioned. To apply, please visit http://iatsat.com.au

What would be the Registration Process?

Students have to fill & Submit the online form which is accessible on the website mentioned above. The test Fee has that have to be paid online. At the time of successfully completion of the online registration, you will receive the confirmation mail for the same. And, student will be presented with the admit card along with Centre Information for the examination within 3 weeks of online registration.
Note: You must be able to bring one and the same identity which you have attached during your Registration.

Who can give the IATSAT global exam?

This test is for aspirants who has completed their Diploma, 10+2(senior secondary) or graduation for further study Criteria for qualifying the test: 10+2 with 60% aggregate for this test for all aspirants. For master course you must have to complete 10+2 & graduation with 60% aggregate from top college or university.
Note: Medical students will not be eligible to appear in scholarship programme this year. Students will be permitted maximum two times to be seated for the test and will not acceptable the same at third time. For master courses, you must have completed 10+2 & Graduation along with a minimum of 60% aggregate scores from top college and universities. A gap of 1 year during studies can be accepted but need to well-justify during the visa process.
