IATSAT Olympiad

Olympiad

We (IATSAT) know, alone we can't achieve everything and that is why we believe in collaboration.
Every child in the world has right to get best of education and so, we are planning to collaborate with most of the prestigious universities and colleges along with education institutions for research and development and mentorship which will lead to communities growth.

IATSAT is introducing “Olympiad at Four different categories:

  1. 1. For class 3rd to 9th - IATSAT Junior Olympiad.
  2. 2. For class 10th, 11th & 12th Student - IATSAT young minds Olympiad.

To help students identify their real potential to survive in the competitive world, we are glad to announce”:

  1. 3. National Hospitality &Management (NH&M) Olympiad.
  2. 4. IATSAT Liberal arts & science Olympiad “For graduates and post Graduates.
  3. 5. IATSAT science & Technology Olympiad For graduates and post Graduates.

    4. The best part about the Olympiad is, Along with giving students the taste of competitive edge of the real world”, We will be giving them Exciting Prizes:

  4. 1. Winner - INR 1.5 Lakh and IATSAT trophy and
  5. 2. Runner up - 75K and Trophy.

Guidelines to Participate in Olympiad

  1. 1. There will be only 6 teams in Finale.
  2. 2. Registration Fee: 1000/ each participant.
  3. 3. Skills which Olympiad quest will be testing:
  4. a. Listening skills
  5. b. Arithmatic
  6. c. Logical Reasoning
  7. d. Verbal Ability
  8. e. General Knowledge
  9. f. Life Skills
  10. g. Marketing skill (Ability to sell yourself)

The test will ensure that while you are doing preparation for the Olympiad, the preparation will happen for real life exam as well. There will be total of 6 rounds and elimination will happen after each round. Initial 2 rounds will be conducted in school itself.

Olympiad will be conducted at 4 different levels:

  1. Level 1: Quiz will be conducted at school and we will be selecting 2 winner team from each school.
  2. Level 2: All the selected teams from school will now compete at state level.
  3. Level 3: Teams selected from each state will compete at zonal level.
  4. Level 4: We will be selecting 2 teams from each zone who will be given chance to compete at National Level.

  5. 1. Winner and Runner team selected at national level will be rewarded with Prize money, IATSAT Trophy and certificates.
  6. 2. Benefits for Winner and Runner Up team
  7. 3. They will get the opportunity to be IATSAT Ambassador for 2 years and will get free mentorship, Training from unmatchable subject matter expert.
  8. 4. All the study material from IATSAT will be accessible to them at a very discountable price.
  9. 5. While applying for job, they will receive preference from our end.
  10. 6. Passes for any conference across the globe will be accessible to them at a quite discounted price.
    Discount will also be available to access our online portal.
  11. 7. And the Best part is, International Mentorship will be provided to them for free for 2 yrs and they can directly reach out and interact with our mentoring team.
